Destiny 2

This is online action RPG game. Destiny 2 is free and available on all platforms.

Destiny 2 world have a three playable races:

  • Human
  • Exo
  • Awoken

and more other unplayable.

In each race you can choose class of your Guardian:

  1. Warlock
  2. Titan
  3. Hunter
